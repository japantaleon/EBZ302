Yay or Nay?

Is this right for you? The nature of a CD is that you can't take out the money because you lock it in for a couple years. CD's are great but maybe not for you for whatever reason. Below are three main disadvantages that may help you decide.

Disadvantages

Interest

The interest rates are about 2% so not high at all. Meaning, it would benefit on large investment only.

Penalties

If you decide to take out money from your CD before the amount of years you locked it in for, you will be penalize.

Money

Some banks require a minium deposit. Meaning, you will have to have a decent amount saved up!
