ROTH IRA

Disadvantages

Yay or Nay?

Is this right for you? The nature of a ROTH IRA is that you won't see the money for a very long time but you will after 59 1/2 of age. ROTH IRA's are great but maybe not for you for whatever reason. Below are three main disadvantages that may help you decide.

Disadvantages

Age

There will be a lot of waiting for the money. You can't take it out without penalties until 59 1/2 of age.

Penalties

If you decide to take out money from your ROTH IRA before 59 1/2, you will be penalize heavily.

Money Limit

The limit to which you can contribute to your account is $5,500 annualy no more than that!
